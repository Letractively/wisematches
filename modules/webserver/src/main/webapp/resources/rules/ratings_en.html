WiseMatches uses ELO Rating System for rating calculation. At the end of each game rating of each player in game is recalculated according to this system.

  1. Initial Rating

    1. When new account is created player got predefined rating 1200.

  2. Changing Rating

    1. When a game is rated ratings of all players are changed at the game ends.

    2. If player play with more than one opponent her/his rating is calculated like in mini tournament as if he/she play two or more games. Although such games has only one winner, player may won one opponent and lose other and his/her ratings will be changed accordingly.

  3. K-value

    1. The maximum possible adjustment per game (sometimes called the K-value) was set at K = 16 for masters (who's rating large than 2200) and K = 32 for weaker players.

  4. Not Rated Games

    1. Game is not rated if all players have made less than 3 moves.

    2. All games are not rated for Guest player.

  5. Rating of Robots

    1. All robots have predefined ratings:

      1. 800 for Dull Robot

      2. 1200 for Stager Robot

      3. 1800 for Expert Robot

    2. Robots ratings are not never changed.
