Depending on how much anonymity you want to preserve in game, you may wish to base your username on your real name or a familiar nickname, or use a pseudonym which you do not use elsewhere.

If you choose not to use your real name, you should pick a username that you are comfortable with, but also one that others are comfortable with, and which does not interfere with the project. A controversial name may give a bad impression to other users, and avoiding this is in your own interest.

WiseMatches usernames are case sensitive.

WiseMatches does not allow usernames that are misleading, promotional, offensive or disruptive:

  • Usernames which give the impression that your account has permissions which it does not have (e.g. Administration, Developer, WiseMatches Owner)

  • Vile, profane, rude, or racist names including common swear words, anatomical references, racial slurs, and homonyms of these words.

  • Names of religious, occult, or significant historic origin (e.g. Jesus, Allah, Satan, Stalin, Hitler, Devil, Demon).

  • Trademarked names of products, services, or concepts (e.g. Marlboro, Sony, MacDonald's)

  • Names of famous people, either fictional or non-fictional (e.g. BillClinton, AustinPowers, BritneySpears, HarryPotter).

  • Combinations of words that produce an offensive result (e.g. Hugeaz, TugMcGroin).


For all of the above, misspellings and alternative spellings of the words are also unacceptable.

Usernames that are inappropriate in another language are still considered inappropriate.

The line between acceptable and unacceptable user names is based on the opinions of other editors.


All user names that fall under any of the categories above will be suspended and/or deleted immediately, without any prior notice, with no refund of any kind. Accounts cannot be renamed.
